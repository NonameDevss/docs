## Why background removal quality matters

Background removal is not only about cutting out an object. It also affects edge quality, natural hair details, shadows, and the final look in your design.

## Simple workflow

1. Upload a clear source image.
2. Remove the background.
3. Review edges at 200% zoom.
4. Export as PNG if you need transparency.

## Tips for better results

- Use high-contrast photos when possible.
- Avoid heavily compressed images.
- Keep lighting even around the object.
- If edges look rough, start from a larger source image.
